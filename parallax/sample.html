BEACH BOAR has evolved through inspiration and knowledge in racket sports, fashion, design and craftsmanship.

This unique bat is a result from cutting edge technology and industrial design and has undergone extensive testing and refinements to ensure a high quality product.

High performance

The BEACH BOAR bat is a high performance product with excellent features. The materials are carefully chosen and tested for maximum balance and game control. Form transition between the blade and the grip allows the player to move the hand forward for accurate short game play.

The weight is carefully calculated resulting in the specific positioning of the centre of gravity when combined with the composite blade.

Superior grip

The grip is made from injection moulded plastic with an optimised density. Grip cross section is super eliptical to allow the player to orientate the paddle in the two main playing positions - the forehand and the backhand. Widening of the grip towards the base assists in orientating the hand for harder shots and long play.

The overgrip is carefully chosen for maximum performance also when wet.

The blade

The blade is made of a 4 mm thick composite material consisting of an low-density polyethylene core and an aluminium alloy sandwich. This means it is possible to achieve the optimal ratio of stiffness versus weight. The properties of the material also offer high rigidity and perfect flatness which results in an accurate playing surface. The composite is CNC machined for superior tolerances and precision shape.

The surface material is anodised to increase the surface hardness and become less prone to scratches and abrasion.

The Frescobol

Challenge

Letâs break down some of the basic features and rules of Frescobol.

1. The game

Frescobol is played in any open area. The aim is to support your partner rather than winning. It is an intense and fun workout which will improve your fitness while sharpening your reflexes.

2. Distance

Two players face one another with the objective of hitting the ball without the ball hitting the ground. The distance should be about 6 meters between the individuals.

3. Time

Count balls played within 3 minutes. Count only when one player hits the ball. If the ball drops the timer is not stopped, and the count continous when the rally resumes.

4. Rally

After 3 minutes check the total number of hits. The number divided by 2 will be given to each player as a rating. In a multiplayer game the player with highest rating wins.
